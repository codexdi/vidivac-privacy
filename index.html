Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

VidiVac ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how VidiVac handles your information.

Information We Collect

VidiVac does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All video processing happens entirely on your device.

Photo Library Access

VidiVac requests access to your Photo Library to scan and display your videos, and to save compressed versions back to your library. Your photos and videos are never uploaded to any server. All compression is performed locally on your device.

In-App Purchases

VidiVac offers a one-time in-app purchase ("VidiVac Pro") processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or have access to your payment information. All transactions are handled by Apple.

Analytics and Tracking

VidiVac does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. We do not track your usage or behavior.

Data Storage

VidiVac stores minimal preferences (such as your Pro purchase status and compression count) locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data is not synced or transmitted anywhere.

Children's Privacy

VidiVac does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is rated 4+ and does not contain any objectionable content.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
